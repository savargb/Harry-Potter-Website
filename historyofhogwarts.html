Hogwarts: A History

The Castle is a stronghold of ancient magic built when magic was feared by muggles.

The Four Founders (c. 990 AD)

Hogwarts was founded over a thousand years ago by the four most brilliant witches and wizards of their time: Godric Gryffindor, Helga Hufflepuff, Rowena Ravenclaw, and Salazar Slytherin. They built this castle together, far from prying muggle eyes, to create a safe-haven for magical education.

The Triwizard Tradition

A magical contest held between the three largest wizarding schools of Europe: Hogwarts, Durmstrang, and Beauxbatons. It was discontinued in 1792 due to its high death toll until its revival in 1994, which famously saw the return of the Dark Lord.

The Restricted Section

Home to books of powerful Dark Magic and advanced enchantments. Entrance is strictly forbidden without a signed note from a professor. Many of these volumes are capable of physical harm to the unprepared reader.

The Battle of Hogwarts

The final conflict of the Second Wizarding War, occurring on 2 May 1998. Students, teachers, and members of the Order of the Phoenix made their final stand against Lord Voldemort and his Death Eaters to secure the future of the wizarding world.
