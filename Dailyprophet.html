The Daily Prophet

Breaking News

Mass Breakout from Azkaban!

LONDON — The Ministry of Magic confirmed in the early hours of this morning that a catastrophic security breach has occurred at Azkaban Fortress. Ten "high-security" prisoners—among them some of the most depraved criminals ever to be sentenced by the Wizengamot—have vanished from their cells. Minister for Magic Cornelius Fudge appeared visibly shaken when he addressed reporters at an emergency press conference in the Atrium.
"We have a strong suspicion that the breakout was led by none other than the notorious mass-murderer Sirius Black," Fudge stated, clutching his lime-green bowler hat. "It is our belief that Black, using the same dark methods he employed to escape two years ago, orchestrated this mass exodus to rally what remains of his former master's fanatical followers."

THE ESCAPEES: A ROGUES' GALLERY
The Ministry has released the names of the "Inner Circle" fugitives. The public is warned that these individuals are extremely dangerous and should not be approached under any circumstances:

  • Antonin Dolohov: Convicted of the brutal murders of the Prewetts.
  • Augustus Rookwood: A former Ministry Unspeakable turned spy.
  • Bellatrix Lestrange: Sentenced to life for the torture of the Longbottoms.
  • Algernon Rookwood & The Mulcibers: Experts in the Imperius Curse.

DEMENTORS: LOYAL GUARDS OR SILENT ALLIES?


While Dementors are currently "patrolling the perimeter" of the island fortress, many are asking how ten wizards—weakened by years of exposure to the Dementor's Kiss—could possibly bypass the most stringent magical protections in the world. Sources within the Department of Magical Law Enforcement suggest the Dementors may have simply allowed the prisoners to walk out. When asked for comment, the Ministry dismissed such "scaremongering" as the work of Albus Dumbledore and his agitators.


WHAT THIS MEANS TO YOU:


A state of emergency has been declared. The Ministry has issued the following mandates:
  • CURFEW: All wizarding households are advised to be behind locked doors by sundown.
  • FLOO WATCH: The Floo Network is being monitored for "irregular travel signatures."
  • REWARD: A bounty of 10,000 Galleons is offered for information leading to the capture of any escapee.
