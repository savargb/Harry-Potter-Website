The Daily Prophet

VOL. CCXVIII... NO. 421 LONDON: FRIDAY PRICE: 5 KNUTS
Breaking News

Mass Breakout from Azkaban!

LONDON — The Ministry of Magic confirmed in the early hours of this morning that a catastrophic security breach has occurred at Azkaban Fortress. Ten "high-security" prisoners have vanished from their cells.

FUGITIVE SPOTTED: Bellatrix Lestrange among the escapees.

Minister for Magic Cornelius Fudge stated: "We have a strong suspicion that the breakout was led by none other than the notorious mass-murderer Sirius Black."

The Escapees: A Rogues' Gallery

  • Antonin Dolohov: Convicted of the murders of the Prewetts.
  • Augustus Rookwood: Former Unspeakable.
  • Bellatrix Lestrange: Sentenced for the torture of the Longbottoms.
Ministry Mandates

CURFEW: All households advised to be behind locked doors by sundown.

REWARD: 10,000 Galleons for any information leading to capture.
